These range from straightforward (Library, Restaurant) to moderately complex (E-commerce, University, Hospital). Each has enough detail to create a meaningful ER diagram with various relationship types:

* One-to-many: Customer → Orders, Department → Courses
* Many-to-many: Products ↔ Categories, Books ↔ Authors, Students ↔ Sections
* One-to-one: Department → Head Professor
